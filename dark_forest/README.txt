Overview:
This game is inspired by the original Slenderman game. The environment 
features a dark forest with trees, bushes, grass, mountains, and a small 
cabin that the player can enter for shelter. A crow flies in circles 
above the cabin to add atmosphere. The player can move around the scene 
and interact with spinning coins placed throughout the forest. When a 
coin is clicked (with the cursor unlocked), it disappears and a short 
sparkle effect is shown. Two lighting options are available: a 
flashlight and a point light inside the cabin.

Controls:
Arrow keys / WASD – Move forward, backward, left, right

F – Toggle flashlight

P – Toggle point light inside the cabin

Space – Lock/unlock the mouse cursor

Left click – Pick up coins (only works when the cursor is unlocked)
